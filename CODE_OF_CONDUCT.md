# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We commit to a respectful, harassment-free collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but make risks explicit.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Respect privacy and security boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or secrets.
- Abusive, discriminatory, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or contributors.
